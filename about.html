I return care to the community, always open to collaborating and creating spaces and media that is for the community and enables people to feel seen, included and heard in an exciting way. I use design as a tool for social change and impact, utilising visual culture as a medium for furthering my, and my client’s motives. I enjoy experimenting and pushing limits, working with mixed media and creating with a powerful idea. I am innovative, insightful and dynamic. I am curious about the growing intersection of art and technology and human computer interaction. I am inspired by cyber feminism, and collective consciousness. I am excited by underground cultures and opposing the big daddy mainframe.

Bio (short):

Cynetic studios is a design studio based in Naarm (MELB) run by Hannah Ogawa. With a particular focus on innovative and insightful solutions, Cynetic Studios aims to utilise ideas to create powerful design for social impact. This has shaped into many mediums including interactive installations, branding concepts, work for productions and events as well as new media artworks. Shortened to Cy_, Hannah also DJs and produces music, with a particular focus on sound design and immersive soundscapes through atmospheric sound. Hannah Ogawa is also an actress, performing for film and TV. These multi-disciplinary practices allow Cynetic Studios to be dynamic and encompassing, with skills from multiple disciplines and a deep understanding of various creative practices.

Bio (DJ):

CY_ is a DJ and artist working in the experimental and genre-bending spheres of creative practice. Taking influences from deconstructed club, breakbeats and the Asian underground music scene, Cy_ blends immersive soundscapes with intricate sound design to keep the dance floor alive and pumping. With community and Queer BIPOC in mind, Cy_ makes noise for the community through their music and event planning. Having grown up in the doof scene, Cy_ takes inspiration from underground culture in their atmospheric and exciting mixes.
